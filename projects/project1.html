Rachmaninoff Runner

Description

I've created a game using Python's PyGame package. In the game, you have to help Rachmaninoff avoid obstacles such as teddy bears (symbolizing his tough childhood), groups of people (representing harsh critics), ghosts (standing in for his sisters who passed away from anemia), and even his own struggles with depression and disease. The game starts with Rachmaninoff standing in front of a coffin, just like in the dream he had that supposedly inspired his famous composition "Prelude in C Sharp Minor." The game's intro takes place in the Kremlin, and the background mirrors the music during Section B of the Prelude. The background moves chaotically while the obstacles shake to represent the repeated triplets in the music.
